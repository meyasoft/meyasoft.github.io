Your Privacy is Our Priority

Colic Baby is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1 Information Collection

Colic Baby is a white noise application designed to help babies sleep. We are committed to protecting your privacy and have designed our app to operate without collecting any personal data.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Device identifiers or unique IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any form of user-generated content

2 App Permissions

Our app requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:

  • Foreground Service: Enables audio playback when the app is in the background or screen is off
  • Wake Lock: Keeps the device awake while playing soothing sounds
  • Post Notifications: Displays playback controls in the notification area

3 Data Storage

All audio files and application resources are stored locally on your device. The app does not transmit any data to external servers, and we do not maintain any databases containing user information.

4 Third-Party Services

Colic Baby does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information in the first place.

5 Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be used by parents and caregivers to help babies sleep. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or anyone else. The app is safe and appropriate for use around children of all ages.

6 Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our app. Your privacy is inherently protected by our no-data-collection policy.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8 Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information for you to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over the app and can uninstall it at any time without concern for data retention.
